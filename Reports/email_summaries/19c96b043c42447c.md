###Care allowance coming soon 


The care allowance is sent out every year. With Doccle, you don’t need to worry: as soon as the document is available, you can easily track and pay it from your account.

###WorkID: 1 profile for multiple temp agencies


WorkID is a platform that simplifies both the search for a new job and the related administrative process, primarily for temporary workers, students, and flexi-job workers. 

Recently, WorkID introduced a convenient “Apply with WorkID” feature, allowing you to apply with just one click at a growing number of recruitment agencies. The feature is already available at Accent, Impact and Talentus. Discover all WorkID partners at [www.workid.be](http://www.workid.be/ "https://workid.be/en/what-is-workid"). 

**How does it work? **

Thanks to your Doccle account, you already have access to WorkID. You only need to complete your basic details in WorkID. This ensures that your new employer immediately receives all the necessary information to handle your administrative paperwork correctly.


[ More about WorkID](https://doccle.be/en-be/workid/ "More about WorkID")
