IMPORTANT NOTICE

 

Why we are discontinuing Thorne

You may already have noticed that we have chosen to end our collaboration with Thorne, a brand that has been with us since Greatlife first started. The background is as follows:

Over the years, Thorne has been sold to various financial companies. Most recently, things appear not to have gone very well, and the distributor for Thorne has now abruptly ended its relationship with the company because the brand has unfortunately begun to compromise too much on quality, with hidden ingredients, lower-quality raw materials, and unclear origin. This previously outstanding brand is sadly no longer what it once was.

We will therefore sell the remaining stock we currently have, which consists of earlier batches that still meet good quality standards. After that, we will no longer bring in additional products from Thorne. Greatlife should be associated with the absolute highest quality, and unfortunately Thorne no longer fits that standard as it stands today.

The good news is that we have already developed replacements for many of the Thorne products that are now being removed from our assortment. Because we are extremely meticulous about quality and develop the best products available, you will not need to compromise—quite the opposite. In many cases, you will receive alternatives that surpass the level Thorne previously maintained. In addition, our supplements come in dark glass jars instead of white plastic. These allow less light exposure and contain no microplastics or endocrine-disrupting substances.
