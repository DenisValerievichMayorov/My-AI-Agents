Introducing Gemma 4



Join us in welcoming the newest addition to the Gemma model family: Gemma 4.

‌


Gemma 4 is Google's most intelligent family of open models, delivering the  
reasoning and agentic capabilities of much larger models in a footprint  
small enough to run locally. Released under a Apache 2.0 license, Gemma 4  
empowers developers to deploy state-of-the-art AI anywhere.

‌


Try the models today on Google AI Studio or in the Google AI Edge Gallery,  
no setup required.





Learn about Gemma 4




Byte for byte, the most capable open models



Developed from Gemini 3 research and shaped by community feedback, we  
wanted Gemma 4 to maximize intelligence-per-parameter in sizes that can run  
anywhere—from edge devices to offline developer workstations.
