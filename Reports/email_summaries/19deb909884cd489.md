What is beta-carotene?
Beta-carotene is a natural pigment found in colorful fruits and vegetables such as carrots and sweet potatoes. In the body, it is converted into vitamin A, which helps maintain normal skin.

Beta-carotene is also stored in the skin and may give a light golden tone, while also acting as protection against oxidative stress that can occur with sun exposure.
