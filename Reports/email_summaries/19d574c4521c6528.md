## Content
New analytics, smarter Library images, and stricter spend controls.



 <https://replit.com>



Saturday, April 4, 2026


Hi! I’m Raouf from the Developer Relations team. We shipped application-level 
analytics, brought images into the Library sidebar, and tightened up spending 
controls.

Here’s what’s new.




🖼️ Stock and AI-generated images in Library



 Stock images and AI-generated images now appear in the Library sidebar. You 
can now easily find and reuse any image Agent downloaded or generated without 
going through the file tree.


📊 Application-level analytics



 You can now view analytics at the application level, not just the deployment 
level. This gives you a clearer picture of your app's overall performance, 
including traffic, most visited pages, and response times.


💰 Usage alerts are now usage limits


"Usage alerts" have been renamed to "usage limits" across the platform. This 
isn't just a label change. Limits now block usage when you hit your threshold, 
giving control over your spending.


🔧 More improvements


- Plan mode fix: Plan mode now correctly activates when you toggle it on 
mid-conversation.

- Slide export improvements: Export timeouts now scale for large slide decks, 
supporting 200+ slides.


You can find weekly updates in our changelog <https://docs.replit.com/updates>.
